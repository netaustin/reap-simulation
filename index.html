Welcome to the ReEntry Simulation

This is an interactive simulation that allows you to experience the challenges of reentry after incarceration.

The simulation is based on real-life experiences of people who have been incarcerated and are returning to their communities.

Many complex issues face people who are reentering society:

  • Community supervision, include parole and probation
  • Banking, healthcare, and education
  • Difficulty obtaining employment

These issues can make daily life difficult and can lead to recidivism, or a return to incarceration.

By playing the simulation, you will experience some of the challenges that the formerly incarcerated face.
