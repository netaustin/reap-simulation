Topics for Reflection

Now that you have experienced the simulation, we encourage you to reflect on what you have learned and learn more about the topics of mass incarceration and the challenges to reentry. Here are some questions to consider:

  1. Did you end up going to jail? To the hospital?
  2. Did you get a job or rent your own apartment?
  3. What surprised you most about the simulation? What did you find most confusing or frustrating?

We hope the simulation was a valuable experience for you. We would love to hear about your reaction to it.

The simulation was developed by Austin Smith during the ReEntry Acceleration Program at the Tamer Center for Social Enterprise at Columbia Business School.
